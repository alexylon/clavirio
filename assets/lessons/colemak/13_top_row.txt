quest for joy and happiness uplifts those
the world spins gently around its quiet sun
judge not what you find; pursue your own way
faithful friends gather where the light falls
golden shining fields delight our restless eyes
joyful daughters played outside in the rain
together they questioned why it happened now
good fortune guides the righteous on this day
quiet whispers float upon the westward wind
thoughtful judges and wise leaders guide all
a joyful spirit delights in useful pursuits
their hopeful words gathered a great following
without question she just forged ahead in it
perhaps good insights shall lighten our path
the rightful pursuit of worthy goals is joy
